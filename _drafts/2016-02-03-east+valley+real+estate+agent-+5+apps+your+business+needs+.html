The real estate business has really embraced technology over the last few years. Today we are going to talk about 5 applications that can help you strengthen and grow your real estate business. Here they are.

1. Dropbox
We use this app to store any documents that we are using in a transaction, from the actual contract itself to the inspection and the appraisal. We can give other people access to this as well. It's cloud-based, so it can be accessed anywhere. 

2. Evernote
In Evernote, you can create a specific file for your prospects where they can see the entire transaction from start to finish. You can put notes in the application, of course, but you can also insert video clips and checklists. You can give your prospect access to all of these.



3. F@xburner
This app is a life-saver. With it, you can take a picture of any document, save it to your phone or tablet, and convert it to a .pdf, which you can fax directly from your mobile device. 

4. Shoeboxed
With Shoeboxed, you can take a picture of a receipt, and it will code the receipt for you and put it in your expense report. Every agent should have this app to take receipts and separate them for your P+L documents.

5. Dragon Dictation
Dragon Dictation allows you to use your voice to write a text, email, social media update, or anything in-between. Simply speak, and your text content will appear. Most of us talk more quickly than we type, so this is app is a huge time-saver.
